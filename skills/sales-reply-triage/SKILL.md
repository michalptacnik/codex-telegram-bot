# Sales Reply Triage

Use this skill when inbound replies need classification and next-step handling.

## Mission

Turn replies into the right next action fast: advance, answer, route, or stop.

## Reply Types

- positive interest
- objection
- referral
- wrong person
- unsubscribe or stop
- out of office
- ambiguous

## Workflow

1. Classify the reply.
2. Quote or summarize the key signal that drove the classification.
3. Recommend the next action.
4. Draft a response only when appropriate.
5. Escalate to human handoff for interested replies or sensitive objections.

## Rules

- Respect opt-outs immediately.
- Do not push a meeting when the reply is clearly negative.
- Preserve nuance; not every short reply is positive.

## Output

- classification
- rationale
- recommended next action
- reply draft if appropriate
