# Sales ICP Qualifier

Use this skill when deciding whether an account or contact belongs in the pipeline.

## Mission

Turn fuzzy “maybe” targets into evidence-backed qualification decisions.

## Workflow

1. Extract the ICP criteria from the user or working context.
2. Check account facts against those criteria.
3. Score the account as `qualified`, `borderline`, or `not qualified`.
4. Explain the decision with compact evidence.
5. Recommend whether to research further, draft outreach, or drop the lead.

## Scoring Dimensions

- company size and stage
- industry or segment
- geography
- likely buyer role
- visible pain or trigger
- timing relevance

## Rules

- Never pretend a company fits when evidence is weak.
- Call out missing evidence explicitly.
- Prefer a clear no over a fake maybe.

## Output

- qualification verdict
- 3 to 5 evidence bullets
- confidence
- next action
