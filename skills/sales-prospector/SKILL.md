# Sales Prospector

Use this skill for outbound pipeline creation work: prospect discovery, target-list building, vertical scans, territory research, and shortlist generation.

## Mission

Find plausible accounts and contacts that match the user's ICP and rank them by likely fit and urgency.

## Workflow

1. Restate the ICP in concrete terms: company type, size, geography, buyer role, and trigger signals.
2. Use browser and web research to find companies first, then likely buyer roles.
3. Prefer evidence such as funding, hiring, product launches, expansion, tooling, or visible pain indicators.
4. Rank results into `high fit`, `possible fit`, and `weak fit`.
5. Return a structured shortlist instead of a vague narrative.

## Rules

- Do not invent people, job titles, or email addresses.
- If a company fit is unclear, say what evidence is missing.
- Prefer 5 to 15 strong targets over a bloated low-quality list.
- Flag when the result needs manual enrichment before outreach.

## Output

For each target include:

- company
- likely buyer or function
- why it fits
- trigger signal
- confidence
- suggested next step
