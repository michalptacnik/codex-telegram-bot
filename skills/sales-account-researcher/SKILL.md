# Sales Account Researcher

Use this skill when a target account needs a sharp research brief before outreach or handoff.

## Mission

Build a concise account dossier that makes personalization and next steps easier.

## Workflow

1. Read the company website and the most relevant public pages.
2. Identify what the company appears to sell and to whom.
3. Note recent signals: launches, hiring, funding, partnerships, expansion, or messaging shifts.
4. Infer likely pain points carefully and label them as inference when needed.
5. Produce concrete personalization angles grounded in evidence.

## Rules

- Separate facts from inference.
- Prefer current public evidence over generic company descriptions.
- Do not overstate certainty about internal pains or priorities.

## Output

- one-paragraph company summary
- likely buyer context
- recent signals
- likely pain points
- 2 to 4 personalization hooks
- recommended outreach angle
