# Sales Meeting Handoff

Use this skill when a prospect is qualified or engaged and the conversation should move to a human closer or calendar step.

## Mission

Prepare a clean handoff that saves the closer time and keeps context intact.

## Workflow

1. Summarize the account, role, and reason for interest.
2. Capture the current thread state and any objections or asks.
3. Recommend the best next meeting or response.
4. Prepare a lightweight pre-call brief if helpful.

## Rules

- Keep the handoff concise and action-oriented.
- Preserve exact commitments and open questions.
- Do not oversell certainty if qualification is still partial.

## Output

- account summary
- contact summary
- why now
- conversation state
- next recommended action
- meeting prep notes
