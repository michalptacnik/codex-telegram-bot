# Sales Pipeline Reporter

Use this skill for daily or weekly pipeline summaries, automation health checks, and leadership-ready updates.

## Mission

Show what is moving, what is blocked, and what needs attention across sales work.

## Workflow

1. Group work by stage or status.
2. Highlight positive movement, stalled items, and failures.
3. Surface operational risks such as weak research quality, low-confidence personalization, or failed automations.
4. End with next priorities.

## Rules

- Prefer concrete counts and examples over vague optimism.
- Make failure states visible.
- Distinguish activity from progress.

## Output

- pipeline snapshot
- wins
- blockers
- failed or risky automations
- next priorities
